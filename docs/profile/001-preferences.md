# Preferences

Contains my free-form job search preferences. Do not treat this file as a final job search query or screening checklist.

## Position preferences

- Backend is the primary target.
- Full-stack is acceptable if backend work is substantial.
- Frontend is acceptable but lower priority.
- Team or tech leadership is acceptable.

## Stack preferences

- TypeScript is required.
- Other programming languages are acceptable if they are secondary, not core to the role, and the company is willing to give me time to learn them.
- Node.js or Bun is required if we are talking about a role that includes backend work.
- React, Angular or Vue are required if we are talking about a role that includes frontend work.
- A role is not suitable if:
  - It requires mandatory technical skills that are not listed in `004-skills.md`.

## Company preferences

- Outstaffing companies are excluded.
- Intermediary companies, such as Toptal or Proxify, are excluded.
- Companies engaged in deceiving users are excluded.
- This includes scammy products, deceptive subscriptions, dark patterns, and aggressive misleading marketing.
- Other companies are acceptable.

## Legal and visa constraints

- I have a work permit for employee roles in Serbia.
- Outside Serbia, employee setup requires visa/work permit sponsorship unless I already have the legal right to work in that country.
- B2B is possible internationally, subject to the legal entity constraints below.
- I am not ready to work with a Russian legal entity in any form.
- If a company is Russian, it must have a non-Russian legal entity.
- Any uncertainty around citizenship-based restrictions should be checked manually.

## Relocation availability

I am ready to relocate to:

- Any EU country, except Bulgaria, Cyprus, Slovakia, Slovenia, Romania, Greece.
- UK.
- US.

I need relocation and visa support.

## Work format availability

- In Serbia, remote, hybrid, and on-site roles are acceptable.
- Outside Serbia, remote, hybrid, and on-site roles are acceptable if relocation support is provided.
- Outside Serbia, only remote roles are acceptable if relocation support is not provided.

## Legal work setup

- In Serbia, both employee and B2B setups are acceptable.
- Outside Serbia, both employee and B2B setups are acceptable if relocation support is provided.
- Outside Serbia, only B2B setup is acceptable if relocation support is not provided.

## Language preferences

- Local language requirements are not acceptable unless English is sufficient.

## Compensation preferences

- Monthly compensation must not be less than EUR 4,500 before taxes.
- The preferred monthly compensation is EUR 5,000 or higher before taxes.
